An Early Win

What you are about to see is the first lesson of the first course of this Nanodegree.

You should complete this lesson in a few days if you can. Throughout this lesson you will be taking notes on what you're learning. Afterwards, you will convert your notes to simple HTML (like what you're reading now) and then submit them for personalized feedback from a Udacity reviewer.

Personal feedback is one of the best parts about taking a Nanodegree, and we want you to experience it as early in the program as possible.

Try your best to complete this lesson (and Stage 0 which comes after), within a week of signing up!
